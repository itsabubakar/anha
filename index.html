ANHA

AFRICAN NONI AND HERBS ASSOCIATION

ANHA is an umbrella Association for researchers, botanists, horticulturists, naturopaths, farmers/growers, processors, and marketers of Noni and other herbs.

Our Association protects the interests of members in the entire value chain of Noni and other herbs.

We are chiefly concerned with policies, standardization, trainings and capacity building, processing, fair trade, packaging, marketing and sales as it relates to Noni and other herbs.
