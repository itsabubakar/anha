African Herbs and Noni Assosiation

Africa is home to an extensive and diverse medicinal plant life. This includes commonly used herbs like Rooibos (Aspalathus linearis), Devil’s claw (Harpagophytum procumbens), Buchu (Agathosma betulina), Cape Aloe (Aloe ferox) and Hoodia (Hoodia gordonii).

These plant - or herb-based treatments have been a key part of the continent’s traditional medicinal practices for thousands of years

In some areas, traditional treatments are the main or only treatment because they are accessible, affordable and culturally accepted.

Numerous traditional African medicines are undeniably beneficial in treating disease or maintaining good health. Some have even been the source of many prescription medicines.

Major obstacles to the use of African medicinal plants are their poor quality control and safety.

African Herbs and Noni Association is an association determined to research the most beneficial and efficient ways to use these herbs to improve health and general well being.

African Herbs and Noni Association is an association aims to provide a standard way for growing, preserving and packaging of the herbs. It also serves as a market place for buying high quality herbs

Our Vision

To be one of the leading Associations in Africa for the promotion of Noni and other Herbs’ competitive products in line with global best practice.

Our Mission

To create awareness on the many benefits of Noni and other herbal products through the development of a robust value chain.

Core Values

Ethics, Innovation, Standardisation and Accountability.
